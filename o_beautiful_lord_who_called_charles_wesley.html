O Beautiful Lord Who Called Charles Wesley

O Beautiful Lord, who called Charles Wesley
to bless the world with songs of salvation,
use these humble offerings to strengthen our devotion and worship,
that we may sing the music of your mercy,
and count the cost of our redemption,
through Christ our Lord. Amen.

Text: Eric D Nelson
